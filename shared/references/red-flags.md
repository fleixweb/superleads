# Red Flags

Block or remediate before formal delivery when any of these appear:

- Guessed email or source-less contact in an exportable field.
- Search result summary written as a verified company fact.
- Hypothesis written as Claim or used as Assessment qualification evidence.
- Obvious company identity mismatch or wrong domain.
- Contact assigned to a company without source context.
- Inaccessible, blocked, login-wall, or hidden content used as evidence.
- Delivery output lacks status explanations for weak evidence.
- Audit graph hash is stale.
- Critical or major ReviewFinding remains open.
- A positive customer has no current-Brief in-scope decision backed by
  same-Entity formal ClaimEvidence for every required direction rule.
- An exclusion signal supported by public evidence is ignored, or an unknown
  critical direction rule is presented as a match.
- A competitor, brand, manufacturer, or reference seed enters a customer table
  without current-task permission and direction evidence.
- A keyword, complete contact record, country match, mail, image, OCR, or
  Candidate clue is used as a substitute for the current development boundary.

Downgrade but do not block when the issue is simply weak evidence that is clearly labeled.
